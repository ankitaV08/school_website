Our Mission & Vision


We, the members of Carmel Education family animated by our Guru Jesus Christ the teacher par excellence, commit ourselves to competence, contemplation and character to form efficient, effective, empowered, enriched, spirit filled Carmelites who strive for excellence in knowledge and skill to lead and serve mankind for their own fulfillment and the progress of humanity. [Read More...]






Our Inspiration


Our inspiration is Blessed Kuriakose Elias Chavara, the founder Father of the congregation of the Mother of Carmel which manages the institution.He was a man of God and a pioneering visionary of education in Kerala in the 19th century. He was versatile genius. vibrant educationalist in Kerala in the 19th century. He brought about effective pedagogical tradition breaking the barriers of illiteracy, casteism, injustice and corruption and radiating the effulgence of knowledge, wisdom, goodness and godliness. Awards & Scholarships


Attractive prizes are given to one boy and one girl for outstanding performance in sports. Prizes for the best performances in Dance, Music, Debate, Art and other activities are given at the end of the session. Carmel Scholarship instituted by the Management is awarded to the allrounder of the outgoing batch every year. Mr. A Savari Muthu memorial prize sponsored by Rev. Fr. Abraham Michel is given to the students in recognition to their meritorious academic performance along with good behaviour, discipline, cleanliness, punctuality etc.. [Read More...]
